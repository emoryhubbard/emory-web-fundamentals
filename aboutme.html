About Me

Emory Hubbard

I'm currently majoring in web design and development with the goal to become a web developer and software developer.

I'm minoring in Illustration to get a great foundation in design and drawing to learn how to make good design, layouts, and assets for websites, apps, and/or games that I will develop. I'm learning to have a passion for art now that I've begun taking classes in it. Thanks to some tender mercies from Heavenly Father a former weak point is on its way to becoming a strength. My wife Amanda is an amazing illustrator and she has been helping me and inpsiring me a lot in this field. I chose one of her illustrations of us for this page.

I love reading, programming, and going on dates with my wife. I spend a lot of time at work as a delivery driver and I'm a full-time student as well, but when I do have some free time you will find me spending time with family. I hope to read the third volume of Saints soon.

I've been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints for 4 years. I served a mission for 2 of those. The Savior found me while I was praying and searching for what God's plan for my life was. I got a phone call from two Sister missionaries who helped me discover that the Lord has called prophets again, brought forth wonderful Scripture, and restored His Church.
